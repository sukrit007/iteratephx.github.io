About

A Day of Inspiration!

Join us for the first DevOps Conference in the Valley of the Sun

September 1st 2015, Phoenix

Discover

what industry leaders and practictioners do to deliver, deploy, and operate software applications.

Share

what works and what doesn't.

Meet others who want to improve software delivery practices.

Try

new frameworks, ideas, and practices.

Through several workshops you will be able to get a feel of already proven as well as cutting edge tools
